Lemonade

Description

When life gives you lemons, yes.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp. coarsely chopped preserved lemon
  • 3 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
  • Chilled tonic water and thinly sliced preserved lemon (for serving)

    • Steps

    1. Crush 3 Tbsp. coarsely chopped preserved lemon to a paste with a mortar and pestle or the flat side of a chefâ€™s knife. Transfer to a small bowl and stir in 3 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice.
    2. Divide mixture among 4 highball glasses. Fill with ice, then top off with chilled tonic water. Garnish with thinly sliced preserved lemon.
